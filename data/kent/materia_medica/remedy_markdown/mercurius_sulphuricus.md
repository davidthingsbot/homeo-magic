# Mercurius Sulphuricus

(Sulphate of Mercury. Turpeth mineral.)
Merc. sulph. will sometimes help, when you have a case of hydrothorax with quick, short breathing, etc.,
and burning of the chest.
If you go to an old case of hypostatic congestion, with dropsy, or a case of dyspnoea from hydrothorax,
when the Merc. base is present. you will be astonished at the action of this sulphate.
